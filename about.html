We Offer High Quality Services

As taking the current situations into view we thought to give a good service by providing information on availability resourses for COVID victims .

We will offer high quality services by providing online information related to covid beds,oxygen cylinders and vaccines .

To Satisfy All Patients

Rising number of COVID-19 cases in India, citizens are scrambling for medical oxygen, hospital beds, antiviral drugs and other supplies. Many have even put out desperate pleas on social media platforms to find COVID-related resources for their loved ones. India's unprecedented rise in COVID-19 cases has surpassed all global records. The second COVID-19 wave has also triggered a shortage of hospital beds, medical oxygen and medicines. .

So Our agenda is to help those in needs by designing a page comprising the details of all resources, including available beds, ventilators, oxygen cylinders, remdesivir medication and other related information .

Helping Needs

The nation is facing a huge crisis with the increasing number of corona cases every minute. The major cities like Delhi, Pune, Mumbai, etc have witnessed a major spike in the Covid-19 cases and deaths in previous days. This has led to increasing in demand for the number of oxygen cylinders, hospital beds, plasma donors, injections, Remdesivir, and other resources.

Hence All we want to do is to rescue the lives of COVID victims by presenting the details of covid beds,oxygen cylinders and plasma from nearby available hospitas and centers by designing a webpage.
